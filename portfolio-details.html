Our Goal

Our team took on the challenge of making an affordable 2 dimensional mapping device utilizing ultrasonic sensors. Our project consists of a pod-like device that is able to have a full 360 degree range with percent error of 2cm. The map generated is open source to the user for their desire of usage. Because our device utilizes ultrasonic waves for localization, we gave it the name Echo. The pods use of ultrasonic sensors has a range of 14ft, which is be able to map most small rooms. Eventually, as we create more echo pods, these pods will be able to communicate with each other creating a larger, much more extensive map. We successfully created a prototype that is able to map out its surroundings in a continuous fashion. Our process consisted of many changes such as remodeling the casing as well as changing from a stepper motor to a continuous servo motor. In order to make these component and structural changes we had to decide over the pros and cons of each decision which eventually updated into the current development we have today
